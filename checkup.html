Oral examination and prophylaxis

A regular dental check-up is crucial to maintain good oral hygiene – keep your teeth and gums healthy . There are two parts to this appointment – oral examination and check-up followed by general cleaning known as oral prophylaxis which is preventative.

How it works?

Intraoral Examination

The dentist will check for cavities, plaque and tartar on your teeth. They will look out for any redness, swelling or infection

Routine Dental X-Ray

A routine X-ray will be taken to spot cavities in between teeth and check for any other signs of trouble

Extraoral Examination

The dentist will check for lymph nodes, the TMJ – joint connecting the jawbone to the skull and facial muscle tenderness for any signs that could indicate a medical condition

Oral Prophylaxis Procedure

This is preventative general cleaning treatment to get rid of the tartar build up in your mouth. It involves scaling, polishing and flossing
