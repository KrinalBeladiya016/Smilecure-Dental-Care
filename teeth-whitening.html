Laser whitening or Teeth Bleaching

Tooth whitening is the process of lightening the colour of teeth without removing any surface of the tooth . It is usually done with Hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide as active ingredients, which are absorbed by the enamel . Laser tooth whitening often gives up to 6 shades whiter teeth.
