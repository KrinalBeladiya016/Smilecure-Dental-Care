Teeth Cleaning

Scaling & root planing (deep cleaning) are dental procedures that involve the removal of stains and hard yellow deposits stuck above & below the gumline & inside the gums . It is important got get this done if your gums, bones, and surrounding tissues have gotten damaged due to gum disease.

How it works?

Local Anaesthesia Administration

The dentist numbs the area to be treated by injecting a drug. This ensures a painless treatment through temporary loss of sensation.

Scaling

Scaling is done manually or using an ultrasonic instrument to loosen plaque and remove hardened deposits around gums. For some the treatment ends here

Root Planing

Complete removal of cementum the calcified substance that covers the root and cleaning deep below the gums where the bones are infected

Post Treatment

The dentist flushes the area to remove any remaining bacteria & applies pressure to ensure proper gums growth.
