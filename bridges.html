Replace your missing tooth with our bridges

A dental bridge is a fixed restoration that replaces one or more missing teeth in your mouth . Dental bridges mimic the look, shape, and function of natural teeth . They are also custom-made for every patient.The dental bridge consists of two dental crowns known as abutments that will cover the healthy adjacent teeth & a pontic to replace the missing tooth.
